STATE MACHINE FLOW:
------------------
FLAT (initial state, can enter trades)
  ↓ onSignal()
SIGNALLED (decision made, building order)
  ↓ onOrderSent()
ORDER_PENDING (order submitted, waiting for fills)
  ↓ onPartialFill() or onFilled()
PARTIAL_FILL (some qty filled, waiting for rest)
  ↓ onFilled()
FILLED (position complete)
  ↓ onExitSent()
EXIT_PENDING (closing position)
  ↓ onFlat()
FLAT (ready for next trade)

GATES ENFORCED:
